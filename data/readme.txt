The data is available at http://neurotycho.org/food-tracking-task (subdural)
To reproduce results from the paper download all 8 datasets:
1) Download the dataset (i.e. 20090611S1_FTT_A_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG32-Motion12) and unpack them. 
2) Browse down the unpacked folders until you reach final folder with Motion.csv and ECoG.csv files inside. 
3) Move this final folder to the data directory. 
After you're done, you should have the following folders in your data dorectory:
20090602S1_FTT_K1_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG64-Motion7
20090525S1_FTT_K1_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG64-Motion8
20090527S1_FTT_K1_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG64-Motion8
20081127S1_FTT_A_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG32-Motion8
20090116S1_FTT_A_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG32-Motion10
20081224S1_FTT_A_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG32-Motion10
20090121S1_FTT_A_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG32-Motion10
20090611S1_FTT_A_ZenasChao_csv_ECoG32-Motion12 

For more information on the data, refer to
Chao ZC, Nagasaka Y, Fujii N (2010). "Long-term asynchronous decoding of arm motion using electrocorticographic signals in monkeys." Frontiers in Neuroengineering 3:3. doi:10.3389/fneng.2010.00003
